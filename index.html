Academic Background

I pursued my AI studies through a collaborative program between Nanjing University of Information Science and Technology and South East Technological University. My coursework included Machine Learning, Digital Image Processing, Data Mining, Data Structures and Algorithms, Cloud Computing, and Database Systems. These courses strengthened my understanding of intelligent algorithms, data-driven decision-making, and system-level computing.

I developed hands-on skills in Python for algorithm implementation and data analysis, utilized MySQL and MongoDB for data management, and gained practical experience with Docker-based containerization through operating systems and cloud computing training.
